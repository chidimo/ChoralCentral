Privacy policy (Last updated: 21st June, 2018)

Information we collect

When you first register an account with ChoralCentral we require that you provide a valid and working email address. After your registration, we have an option for you to include additional information. This is completely optional and is available via the edit profile option.

In sum we collect the following information

  1. Email address (mandatory)
  2. First name (optional)
  3. Last name (optional)
  4. Location (optional)

What we do with your information

We use your email address for the following purposes, among others

  1. To register you in our system when you first sign up (in combination with your password).
  2. To identify you in our system when you return (in combination with your password)
  3. To reset your password in the event that you lose it.
  4. Any other purpose which we deem fit for purposes of making the site better, such as analytics and so on.
